Instructions
1. Make a courier contract with your items to Krab Shack. [KRBSH]
- Do not make contracts with any assembled containers.
2. Enter the appropriate collateral (the cost to replace your items) and reward based on the pricing below. Collateral is REQUIRED.
3. Set "Expiration" to 2 weeks and "Days to complete" to 1 day.
4. Submit and patiently await your delivery.

- If your contract is rejected, you will receive a mail as to why. This may be due to incorrect reward/collateral, prohibited items, etc.
- If you have any questions, join the Discord server and @ me.
